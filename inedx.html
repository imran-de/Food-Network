About Us

Welcome to Food Network. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of Food, with a focus on Healthy and delicious Food. Founded in 2021 by Imran Ahmed, Food Network has come a long way from its beginnings in a Sylhet, Bangladesh. When Iman Ahmed first started out, his passion for providing the best Healthy and delicious Food drove him to do intense research, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming Food store. We now serve customers all over Sylhet Area. We hope you enjoy our Food as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Food Network Team.
